# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

CoreLift LLC takes security seriously. If you discover a vulnerability in any of our repositories, we appreciate responsible disclosure.

**Email:** security@corelift.io

### What to include

- Description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce
- Affected repository and version (if applicable)
- Any proof of concept or supporting evidence

### What to expect

- Acknowledgment within **1 business day**
- Initial assessment within **3 business days**
- We will work with you to understand and resolve the issue before any public disclosure

### Scope

This policy applies to all repositories under the [CoreLift-LLC](https://github.com/CoreLift-LLC) organization.

### Safe Harbor

We will not pursue legal action against researchers who follow responsible disclosure practices and act in good faith.
